Privacy Policy

1. What We Collect

We collect basically nothing. There is no tracking, no profiling, and no data selling. We only store the absolute minimum credentials required to secure your account and sync your configuration: your username, cryptographically hashed passwords, API/license keys, and interface settings (active models, skills, UI theme).

2. Chat & Vision Storage

Your chat messages and processed vision files are synchronized serverside to ensure a seamless 1:1 experience when switching between devices (like moving from desktop to the Lumi mobile apps). Chat logs are fully private and are never shared with third parties.

3. Data Deletion

You have full control over your profile. To request complete removal of your username, license keys, and synchronized chat sessions, contact support via our official channels.

Terms of Service

1. License Tiers & Limits

  • Pro Plan: Limited access to v1 models. Not optimized for heavy programming or automated tool use.
  • Ultra Plan: Lifetime or subscription access with zero quota limits. Includes infinite access to all V1, Flash, Thinking, Search, Omni, and our flagship DeepMind X1/x1.1 models, plus unlimited image/video generation.

2. API Usage & Script Integration

Ultra users are permitted to integrate their API keys directly into third-party editors (like Cursor, OpenCode, VS Code) and personal scripts. Key sharing is permitted for reasonable personal or familial use (up to 3 devices per 24 hours). Public key distribution, resale, or exceeding device limits is prohibited and will trigger rate limits, lockouts, or license suspension.

3. Model Capabilities & Performance

Models like DeepMind X1 feature a 4M token context window and 3T parameters, built for graduate science GPQA reasoning and code repair. x1.1 Flash is optimized for speed, supporting up to 400 t/s via Web and 700 t/s over raw API endpoints.
